01

Battery-as-a-Service Platform

ALVA Auto

Challenge

EV adoption in Indonesia was hindered by high upfront battery costs, creating a significant barrier for mainstream consumers.

Solution

Designed and launched a Battery-as-a-Service (BaaS) model that separated vehicle and battery ownership, making EVs financially accessible to a broader market.

Result

BaaS accounted for 50% of all B2C sales within 3 months of launch, fundamentally shifting ALVA's sales model and accelerating EV adoption.
